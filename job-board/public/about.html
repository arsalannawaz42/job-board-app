About Us

DailyJobsPK ke baare mein

Hum kya karte hain

DailyJobsPK ek job listing platform hai jahan Pakistan bhar ki latest jobs — banks, private companies, aur govt sector se — ek jagah jama ki jaati hain. Hamara maqsad hai job dhoondne walon ka waqt bachana: bina alag-alag websites khangale, sab jobs ka official apply link yahan mil jaata hai.

Ye site kis ne banayi

DailyJobsPK ek individual ke tor par manage ki jaati hai jo daily naye job openings tareeb se ikhattha kar ke post karta hai, taake job seekers ko sahi aur updated information mil sake.

Hum kaise kaam karte hain

Har job listing mein title, company/bank ka naam, location, aur last date to apply di jaati hai. Apply karne ke liye hum seedha official company/bank ki website ya email ka link dete hain — hum khud kisi ki application collect nahi karte.

Hamse rabta

Koi sawal, feedback, ya job submit karwani ho to hamari Contact page par jaayein.
