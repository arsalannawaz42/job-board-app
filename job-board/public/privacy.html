Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

DailyJobsPK ("hum", "hamari website") aapki privacy ki qadar karti hai. Ye Privacy Policy batati hai ke hum kaunsi information collect karte hain aur usay kaise use karte hain.

1. Ham konsi information collect karte hain

Ye website khud koi personal account ya sign-up nahi maangti. Agar aap Contact page se email karte hain, to sirf wahi information hamare paas aati hai jo aap khud bhejte hain (jese naam, email, message).

2. Cookies aur Advertising

Ye website Google AdSense use karti hai taake ads dikhayi ja sakein. Google aur uske partners cookies use karte hain taake aapko relevant ads dikhayein, jo aapki pichli websites ki visits ke bunyad par ho saktay hain.

  • Aap Google Ads Settings (adssettings.google.com) par jaakar personalized ads ko band kar saktay hain.
  • Third-party vendors, jin mein Google shamil hai, cookies use kar ke ads dikhate hain jo aapki pehle ki website visits par mabni hain.
  • Aap apne browser settings se cookies ko manage ya block kar saktay hain.

3. Third-party Links

Hamari website par jo "Apply Now" links diye jaate hain, wo external companies/banks ki official websites ya email par le jaate hain. Un websites ki apni alag privacy policies hoti hain, jinka DailyJobsPK zimmedar nahi hai.

4. Children's Privacy

Ye website 18 saal se kam umar ke bachon ki information jaan boojh kar collect nahi karti.

5. Policy mein tabdeeli

Ye Privacy Policy waqtan faqtan update ho sakti hai. Koi bhi tabdeeli isi page par post ki jaayegi.

6. Rabta

Is Privacy Policy ke baare mein koi sawal ho to Contact page se hamse rabta karein.
